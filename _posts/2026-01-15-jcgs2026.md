Our paper, "[Spatio-Temporal Prediction of Fine-Grained Origin-Destination Matrices with Applications to Ridesharing](/assets/publications/JCGS接收.pdf)" (Authors: Run Yang, Runpeng Dai, Siran Gao, Xiaocheng Tang, Fan Zhou, Hongtu Zhu), has been accepted in the *Journal of Computational and Graphical Statistics (JCGS)*. This work develops novel spatiotemporal prediction methods for fine-grained origin-destination matrices, with applications to ridesharing platform optimization. Congratulations to all authors!

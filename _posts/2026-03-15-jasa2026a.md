Our paper, "[Distributional Off-Policy Evaluation with Deep Quantile Process Regression](/assets/publications/JASA2026a.pdf)" (Qi Kuang, Chao Wang, Yuling Jiao, Fan Zhou), has been accepted at the *Journal of the American Statistical Association (JASA)*. This work advances distributional off-policy evaluation methods using deep quantile process regression. Congratulations to all authors!
